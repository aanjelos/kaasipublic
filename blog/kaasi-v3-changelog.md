            </p>

            <p>

              <a href="https://kaasi.com.lk">kaasi.com.lk</a>.
            </p>
            <p>
              Alongside the app, I've launched a full website to be the
              central hub. Here you'll find:
            </p>
            <ul class="list-disc list-inside">

            <h2>What's New in the App?</h2>
            <p>
              This update isn't just about the website. The app itself has
              also received some significant improvements.
            </p>

            <h3><strong>Track Daily Expense Fluctuations</strong></h3>
            <p>
              While the chart showing your monthly expenses over the last year
              is still there, you can now toggle to a new 'daily expense
              fluctuation graph'. This new view shows the flow of your
              spending within the current month, making it much easier to see
              how your expenses change from day to day.
            </p>

            <h3><strong>Credit Card History Filter</strong></h3>
            <p>
              Easily keep track of what you have paid already and have left to
              pay in the credit card history without clutter with the help of
              the new filters; letting you choose between all/unpaid/paid
              transactions.
            </p>

            <h3><strong>Quality of Life Improvements</strong></h3>
            <p>
              I've also focused on a number of smaller fixes and improvements
              to make your day-to-day experience smoother:
            </p>
            <ul class="list-disc list-inside">
              <li>Fixed several minor bugs and text inconsistencies.</li>
              <li>Improved how the app handles long descriptions for
                transactions, preventing text overflow.
              </li>
              <li>Added a footer with useful links and information.</li>
            </ul>

            <h2>Supporting Kaasi's Future</h2>
