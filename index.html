Privacy Policy

We do not collect any of your personal or financial data. Everything you enter into Kaasi is stored exclusively in your browser's LocalStorage. Your data is never sent to a server for processing or storage.

When you create a backup, the file is downloaded directly to your device. If the Google Drive sync feature is implemented, the app will only have permission to access the specific backup file it creates and nothing else in your Google Drive.

Because all data is stored locally on your device, we strongly recommend you perform regular backups. We do not take responsibility for any data loss that may occur.

By its nature, Kaasi requires browser cookies and local storage to function. These are enabled by default. If you do not agree to this, please clear your browser data for Kaasi and discontinue using the application.

We use Google Analytics on the website (kaasi.com.lk) and the web app (app.kaasi.com.lk) to get a general overview of visitor traffic and user interactions. This service may collect anonymous data such as your device's operating system and browser type. No personal or identifiable financial information is ever collected or shared.
